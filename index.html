FAQs

What's the difference between a web app and a website?

Websites are primarily for delivering information, while web apps are interactive and can perform specific tasks. Think of a company website as a brochure and their online ordering system as a web app.

How much does it cost to develop a mobile app?

Similar to web apps, the cost depends on complexity and features. Simple apps can range from $10,000 to $75,000, while complex ones can reach over $200,000.

What are some popular programming languages for cloud development?

Many languages are suitable for cloud development, depending on the chosen platform and project requirements.
